About Us

BVSLN System Services is a CMMI Institute Partner and Management Consulting Organization operating out of Bangalore, India.

We primarily focus on Process consulting, taking advantage of the Industry recognised frameworks such as CMMI, ISO 9001, ISO 27001 and SSAE18.

We being a team of experienced professionals in the areas of Project Management and Quality Management, strive to add value to the organizations that we partner with, by way of extending our training services in these areas.
